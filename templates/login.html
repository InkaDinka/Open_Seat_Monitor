ASU Open Seat Monitor

Open Seat Monitor solves the problem of needing to continuously check ASU's class search when class registration opens and seats get filled within a matter of minutes. Simply enter an email that you would like to recieve notifications from and add a class by submitting the class term and class number. Your monitored courses will be displayed when you register and log in and you will be emailed when a seat is available. Up to 2 courses can be monitored per user. This site is free and uses ads to keep running so clicking the ads would be much appreciated.
