ASU Open Seat Monitor

Open Seat Monitor solves the problem of needing to continuously check ASU's class search when class registration opens and seats get filled within a matter of minutes. Submit a valid email that you are willing to recieve notifications from and you can add a class to be monitored by submitting the class term and class number. Your monitored courses will be displayed when you sign in and you will be emailed when a seat is available. Up to 2 courses can be monitored per user.
