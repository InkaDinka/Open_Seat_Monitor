About OSM

Open Seat Monitor solves the problem of needing to continuously check the ASU class search when class registration opens and seats get filled within a matter of minutes. Simply enter an email that you are willing to receive notifications from and enter the class information below. Your ASU course will be monitored for open seats you will be emailed when a seat is available. Monitors classes for all semesters.
